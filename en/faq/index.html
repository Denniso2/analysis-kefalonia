Help & answers

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about our services across Kefalonia and Ithaca. Can’t find yours? Just get in touch.

General

Where do you operate?
We serve the whole of Kefalonia and Ithaca, based in Argostoli.
How long have you been in business?
For more than three decades — 30+ years of continuous service.
How do I get a quote?
Call us or email us with a few details about what you need, and we’ll get back to you shortly.

Pest control

Is treatment safe for children and pets?
Yes — we use approved substances and full safety precautions, and advise on any short period to keep children or pets away from treated areas.
Do you work with businesses?
Yes — homes and businesses alike, including shops, restaurants and hotels.

Chemical analysis & wine

What can you analyse?
Drinking and pool water, oil, and must and wine, among other samples.
Do you offer wine analysis?
Yes — oenology is a specialisation. See the Wine & Oenology page.
How long do results take?
One to four days, depending on the test.

Disinfection

What spaces can you disinfect?
Homes, businesses and enclosed spaces, using approved microbicidal substances and an electric nebuliser.
How soon can the space be used again?
We advise a short waiting period after treatment.
